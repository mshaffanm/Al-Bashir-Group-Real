Key Takeaways
Actionable insights from parameter analysis
Customer Lounge — Critical Gap. 57 low-score instances, the most of any parameter. Complaints include poor seating, no cooling (ACs), flies, unhygienic cups, and comparison with Multan & Islamabad dealerships.
On-Time Delivery — Recurring Issue. 36 low scores. Multiple customers reported cars delivered 1–2 days beyond committed time. No systematic reminder or buffer mechanism visible.
Timely Received — 34 Low Scores. Walk-in customers waiting without being attended promptly. Token system and appointment process need standardization.
Work Standard — 30 Low Scores. Mostly tied to body & paint jobs and specific technicians. Training and QC check before delivery recommended.
Careful Listening — Strongest Parameter. Only 22 low scores, avg 9.67. Advisors score consistently well here — a genuine strength to highlight in team reviews.

April 2026 Anomaly ⚠ Alert
Highest volume month, lowest score — root cause analysis
April 2026: 238 surveys · Avg score 9.42 (vs 9.58 overall)
Highest survey volume coincided with lowest satisfaction. Likely causes: peak workload pressure reducing service quality, higher ambient temperature complaints (summer onset), increased wait times, and staffing/capacity constraints.
Heat-related complaints spiked — Multiple reviews mentioned no AC functioning, customers feeling faint, staff also impacted
Lounge satisfaction collapsed — 9 low-score lounge mentions in April alone, highest of any month
Fully recovered in May 2026 — Score bounced to 9.70, highest of all months, confirming issues were operational not structural
